Sweet Lassi

Description:

Lassi is a blended yogurt drink that is very popular in the Northern Parts of India. It is made with blending curd or yogurt with water or milk, salt or sugar and some spices or herbs.

There are many variations of a lassi recipe, including mango lassi, dry fruits lassi, and malai lassi. Here I am giving an easy, energizing and flavorful lassi recipe variations with you.

Ingredients:

  • Curd
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Saffron

Steps:

  1. Take 2 cups of fresh homemade curd in a bowl.
  2. Blend the curd with wired whisk or immersion blender untill it becomes smooth.
  3. Add 4 to 5 tablespoon of sugar and blend agian untill sugar gets dissolved.
  4. Add 1 cup of cold water to it and mix it well.
  5. Add cardamom powder and some strands of saffron.
  6. Enjoy your lassi.
